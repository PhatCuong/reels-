Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Reels Automation handles information when connected to third-party social platforms such as TikTok.

1. Information We Process

The service may process account authorization tokens, video files selected for publishing, captions, post metadata, publishing status, and platform response data required to complete user-requested publishing actions.

2. How Information Is Used

Information is used only to authenticate with connected platforms, upload or publish content requested by the authorized user, keep operational logs, and troubleshoot publishing errors.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data is shared with third-party platforms only as needed to perform user-requested actions, such as uploading a video to TikTok.

4. Data Storage and Security

Access credentials are stored in protected automation configuration or environment variables. Reasonable safeguards are used to reduce unauthorized access.

5. Data Retention

Operational data is retained only as long as needed for publishing, logging, debugging, or compliance with platform requirements. Users may request removal of connected credentials or related operational data.

6. User Control

Users can disconnect platform access at any time through the relevant platform account settings or by removing stored credentials from the automation environment.

7. Contact

For privacy questions or data removal requests, contact the app owner through the repository or website where this page is hosted.
